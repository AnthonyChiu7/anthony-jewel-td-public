HOW TO PLAY 📖
● BUILD: Place gemstone towers on the grid to stop enemies.
● UPGRADE: Upgrades are GLOBAL! Upgrading a tower type enhances all present and future towers of that type.
● SUPPORT: Upgrade the Tech Lab for bonus range, or place Merchants to earn extra gold from nearby kills.
● GOAL: Survive all 10 waves to win.

⚠️ WARNING: From Wave 7, hyper-fast L5 explosive bosses will spawn. Their 5x5 death explosions will permanently break your towers!
